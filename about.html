Emporia, est. 1854

Established in Manchester in 1854 by the visionary Ernest Thomas Pilkington, Emporia has a rich heritage steeped in craftsmanship and quality. Originally a humble workshop, Emporia quickly gained a reputation for exceptional furniture that graced the homes of discerning clients. Over the years, we’ve evolved, embracing innovation while preserving our timeless values. Today, Emporia is a leading online furniture retailer, seamlessly blending tradition with modernity to bring you exquisite pieces that transform spaces into homes. Explore our collection and be part of our storied legacy, where every piece tells a story of elegance and enduring charm.
